About Myself

My name is Ana Heliza, I'm 17 years old and I'm currently studying technology at SENAI Diadema. I've always been very interested in art and technology, two areas that allow me to express my creativity and explore new possibilities. Ever since I was little, I've enjoyed creating and giving shape to my ideas, whether I'm developing websites or working on designs. For me, each project is a way of putting a bit of myself into the world, mixing technique with artistic expression. I'm always looking to improve my skills and learn more, because I know that the combination of art and technology has an incredible power to transform.
